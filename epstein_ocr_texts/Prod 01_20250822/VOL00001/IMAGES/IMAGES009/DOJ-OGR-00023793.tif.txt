Note to Attorneys:
In general, inmates are permitted to possess up to 100 CDs/DVDs of electronic discovery while housed in
general population housing units. There are computers are avatlable on the housing units exclusively for
inmate review of electronic discovery.

include hard drives, flash drives, iPods, MP3 players or like items) is prohibited.
All discs must be labeled clearly with the inmate’s name, register number and federal case number.
The mailing containing the electronic discovery must be labeled in accordance with BOP’s Legal/Special
Mail procedures as described in BOP policy.
