Dear Judge Torres:
The Government submits this letter pursuant to the Court’s order dated March 3, 2021
(Dkt. No. 46). The Government has conferred with counsel for defendants Tova Noel and Michael
Thomas regarding the parties’ availability for trial. In the third quarter of 2021, counsel for Noel
