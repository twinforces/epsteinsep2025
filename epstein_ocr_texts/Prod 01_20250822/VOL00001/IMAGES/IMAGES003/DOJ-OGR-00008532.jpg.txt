3 A stipulation of testimony is an agreement among the parties that, if called, a witness
4 would have given certain testimony. You must accept as true the fact that the witness would
5 have given the testimony. However, it is for you to determine the effect or weight to give
6 that testimony.
7 You also heard evidence in the form of stipulations that contain facts that were agreed to
8 be true. In such cases, you must accept those facts as true.
