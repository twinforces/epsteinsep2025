The Government writes regarding the two issues remaining following today’s charge

jury why the felony conviction was put before them.” Sand Instr. 7-12 cmt. It is useful in cases
in which the prior felony convictions are sufficiently significant that they are an important part of
the defense, and an explanation would aid the jury in evaluating the evidence. In such a case, it
