A. The Massage Room

Jane testified that she was sexually abused in the massage room of Epstein’s New York
house, and she gave detailed testimony about permanent features of the room. Photographs taken
of the massage room specifically corroborate that testimony.”

Jane described the walls of the room: “[I]t was very dark,” “I don’t know if it was painted
dark, but — or maybe that was the lighting, but it sort of had this, like, red mood. And then there
was just a giant black massage table in the middle of it.” (11/30/21 Tr. at 320). More generally,
she testified that the house was decorated with “fabric wallpaper, like red curtains.” (11/30/21 Tr.
at 318).
