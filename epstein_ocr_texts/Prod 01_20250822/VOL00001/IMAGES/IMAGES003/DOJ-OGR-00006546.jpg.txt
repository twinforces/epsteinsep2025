B. Evidence of the Non-Prosecution Agreement is Not Otherwise Admissible for Any
Purpose
The defense argues that evidence of the NPA is nonetheless admissible for impeachment,
specifically to show the bias and interest of P Po (Def. Opp. at 29).
The Court should reject that baseless argument as mere pretext to put the NPA before the jury, in
an attempt to confuse and mislead the jury and argue for jury nullification.
