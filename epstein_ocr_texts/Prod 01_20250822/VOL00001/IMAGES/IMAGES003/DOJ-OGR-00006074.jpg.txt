Sympathy: Oath As Jurors
Under your oath as jurors you are not to be swayed by sympathy or prejudice. You are to

be guided solely by the evidence in this case. It is for you alone to decide whether the
government has proven beyond a reasonable doubt that Ms. Maxwell is guilty of the crimes
charged based solely on the evidence and subject to the law as I have charged you. _—— Commented [CE3]: Adapted from Sand, Instr 2-12 }
