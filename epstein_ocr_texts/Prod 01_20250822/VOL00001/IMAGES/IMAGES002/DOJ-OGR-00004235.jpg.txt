discovery does not identify the alleged criminal conduct.” Bortnovsky, 820 F.2d at 575; see also
United States v. Luna, No. 3:05-CR-58 (SRU), 2006 WL 1668006, at *2 (D. Conn. May 17,
2006) (“[W ]hen faced with ‘mountains’ of discovery or ‘massive disclosures,’ a defendant may
still be unable to prepare for trial and prevent surprise.”).
